"Impulse" by Northlane

Impulse is the eighth song from Northlane’s third full-length album Node, and the third single from the album. Three days after the official album release, Rise Records uploaded the music video to their YouTube channel. The song lyrically talks about the relationship between people and the digital world we live in. With technology being a large part of daily life in the 21st century, this also brings about issues with people connecting with one another on a real, face-to-face level.

"I No Longer Fear The Razor Guarding My Heel (IV)" by $UICIDEBOY$

The gothic yet vintage “I No Longer Fear the Razor Guarding My Heel IV” is the fourth installment of the I No Longer Fear The Razor Guarding My Heel series, released with I Want To Die In New Orleans as the concluding track 14. The installment is largely based around their feelings of doubting that the rap industry is right for them, along with the usual religion-based analogies, and their perpetual feeling of “losing”.

"Don't Know What You Got" by Cinderella

“Don’t Know What You Got (Till It’s Gone)” is a power ballad written and performed by the glam metal band Cinderella, from their second album Long Cold Winter. Released in August of 1988, it was their most successful single, peakeding at number 12 on the US Billboard Hot 100 in November 1988.

"Cheri Cheri Lady" by Modern Talking

"Cheri, Cheri Lady" is the only single released from Modern Talking's second album Let's Talk About Love, and it was the third single in a row that peaked at number one on the German singles chart, after "You're My Heart, You're My Soul" and "You Can Win If You Want". "Cheri, Cheri Lady" was released on 2 September 1985, but peaked at No. 1 in Germany on 14 October 1985.The single spent four weeks at the top and a total of 24 weeks on the singles chart in Germany and eventually went gold, selling well over 250,000 units there. The single was certified silver in France for certified sales of 250,000 units.

"Will He" by Joji

“Will He” is Joji’s melancholy message to an ex-lover who’s found someone else. It’s the first single from In Tongues, his official debut project under this name. Joji first released a snippet of this song in May 2017 at his Boiler Room performance in Los Angeles.

"Stylo" by Gorillaz

“Stylo” is sort of an extended allegory of the negative effects resulting from over-population, pollution, etc. It represents a conversation of sorts between 2D who represents human nature and Stylo, which represents the plastic fakeness of plastic beach. 2D is sort of singing sadly about the way things are going for humans and our inherent mechanization that will ultimately lead to our destruction.

"W.D.Y.W.F.M.?" by The Neighbourhood

“W.D.Y.W.F.M.” is an acronym for “What do you want from me?” and it’s one of the band’s favorite songs from their debut album I Love You. The song goes through the twists and turns of a relationship, leading the narrator into confusion and despair.
